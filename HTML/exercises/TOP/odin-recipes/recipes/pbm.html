Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masale is a rich and creamy dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and a buttery tomato-based gravy. It's a popular dish in Indian cuisine and is often served with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and mix well.
  5. Pour in heavy cream and sprinkle garam masala. Cook for a few minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
